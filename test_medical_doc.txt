Medical Guidelines - Community-Acquired Pneumonia

Introduction:
Community-acquired pneumonia (CAP) is a common infection requiring prompt treatment.

Treatment Recommendations:
1. Outpatient mild CAP: Amoxicillin 1g TID for 5-7 days OR Azithromycin 500mg daily for 3 days
2. Inpatient CAP: Beta-lactam + macrolide combination
3. Severe CAP: ICU admission, IV antibiotics

Risk Factors:
- Age >65 years
- Chronic lung disease
- Immunosuppression
- Smoking history

Diagnostic Criteria:
- Clinical symptoms: Cough, fever, dyspnea
- Chest X-ray confirmation
- Lab findings: Elevated WBC, CRP

Follow-up:
Clinical improvement expected in 48-72 hours.
