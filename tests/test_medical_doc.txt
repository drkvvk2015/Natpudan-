Medical Knowledge Test Document

**Hypertension Management**
- First-line medications: ACE inhibitors, ARBs, CCBs, thiazide diuretics
- Target BP: < 140/90 mmHg (< 130/80 for diabetics)
- Lifestyle modifications: reduce sodium, exercise, weight loss

**Diabetes Type 2 Treatment**
- First-line: Metformin 500-2000mg daily
- HbA1c target: < 7% for most patients
- Monitor for complications: retinopathy, nephropathy, neuropathy
