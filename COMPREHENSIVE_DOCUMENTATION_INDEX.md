## ‚ùì FAQ

### "Why not just use Claude Vision forever?"
**Answer**: You could, but:
- ‚ùå Costs $1,600+/year forever
- ‚ùå Competitors have same accuracy
- ‚ùå Medical data leaves your servers
- ‚ùå Models never improve for YOUR cases
- ‚ùå Vendor lock-in (pricing can increase)

### "How long does Phase 5-7 take?"
**Answer**: 8-12 weeks total
- Phase 5 (Local Vision): Week 1-3
- Phase 6 (Local LLM): Week 4-6
- Phase 7 (Self-Learning): Week 7-12

### "Do we need GPU?"
**Answer**: Recommended but optional
- GPU: 500ms per image, $800-1500 investment
- CPU: 2-3s per image, $0 investment
- Both work; GPU is faster

### "What if Phase 5 fails?"
**Answer**: Zero risk
- Keep Claude Vision as fallback
- Gradual migration (Week 4) lets you measure
- Can revert instantly if needed

### "Will our data stay private?"
**Answer**: Yes!
- Everything runs on-premise
- HIPAA-compliant
- 100% data control
- Zero external servers

### "Can we license our AI?"
**Answer**: Absolutely
- By Month 6, you'll have unique proprietary models
- Other hospitals might want to license it
- New revenue stream possibility
